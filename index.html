Have you ever wanted to learn how to beat-box? Well just follow along with the buttons on this helpful web-page and you too can be a passable beat boxer before long!

The recommended use of the site is to start with "boots", then to quickly move along to "cats", then going back towards the "boots". When you're more comfortable, try it aloud!
